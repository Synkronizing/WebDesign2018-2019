RegEx Crossword Explanation

RegEx Crossword

Top

".*[GAF]*" is looking for 0 or more characters followed by 0 or more G,A, and/or Fs

"(P|ET|O|TEA)*" is looking an 0 or more P, ET, O, TEA. | means or in parentheses

"[RUSH]+" looks for one or more of any of the characters in the brackets R, U, S, H

Bottom

"(NF|FA|A|FN)+" One or more NF, FA, A, or FN

".*(A|E|I).*" 0 or more characters followed by either A, E, or I, then followed with 0 or more characters

"[SUPER]*" 0 or more characters that are S, U, P, E, or R

Left

"[ONE]*[SKA]" 0 or more O, N, E followed by an S, K, or A

".*(RE|ER)" 0 or more characters followed by RE or ER

"A+[TUB]*" one or more As followed with T, U, or B

Right

".*(O|S)*" 0 or more characters followed by 0 or more of O or S

"[^GOA]*" 0 or more characters that aren't G, O, or A

"[STUPA]+" 1 or more characters that are S, T, U, P, or A
